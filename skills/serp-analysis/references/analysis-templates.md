# SERP Analysis -- Analysis Templates

Compact templates for search-result, intent, feature, and ranking-pattern analysis.

## 1. SERP Snapshot

```markdown
## SERP Analysis: "[keyword]"
**Location**: [location] | **Device**: [mobile/desktop] | **Date**: [date]

| Feature | Present | Position / Placement | Opportunity |
|---------|---------|----------------------|-------------|
| AI Overview | Yes/No | [placement] | [citation strategy] |
| Ads / Shopping | Yes/No | [placement] | [commercial pressure] |
| Featured Snippet | Yes/No | [placement] | [format to target] |
| People Also Ask | Yes/No | [placement] | [questions to answer] |
| Local / Image / Video / News Pack | Yes/No | [placement] | [asset need] |
| Knowledge Panel / Sitelinks / Related Searches | Yes/No | [placement] | [entity or IA implication] |
```
